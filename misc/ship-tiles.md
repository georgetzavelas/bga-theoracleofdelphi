## Ship Tiles to image mapping

Ship Tiles are named in the format of ship-tile-[number].png, where number represents the following:

0 - Storage: 2 - At the start of the game, move your Shield 2 steps to the right.
1 - Storage: 2 - At the start of the game, take 1 Equipment Card from the display and draw 1 Oracle Card.
2 - Storage: 4 - You can also "recolor" Oracle Dice in counterclockwise direction. Additionally, your storage capacity is increased by 2.
3 - Storage: 2 - Whenever you take 1 or more Favor Tokens, take 1 more. This also applies to the starting Favor Tokens.
4 - Storage: 2 - Advance all your Gods on the God Track to the row showing the number of players participating in the game. After using a Special Action of a God, return it to that row instead of the lowest row.
5 - Storage: 2 - Your Ship’s range is increased by 2.
6 - Storage: 2 - Return a Zeus Tile of your choice to the box. You do not receive its reward. You require 11 completed tasks to win the game instead of 12.
7 - Storage: 2 - Your cost for "recoloring" Oracle Dice is reduced by 1.
